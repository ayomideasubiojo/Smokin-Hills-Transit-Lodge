ABOUT US

ABOUT

Smokin Hills Transit Lodge is the most preferred hotel in Nigeria due to its golf resort. It is located on the outskirt of the quiet town of Ilara Mokin, 20 minutes drive from Akure the capital city of Ondo state. We offer our corporate clients and walk in guests a perfect blend of relaxation, where our modern style meets unquestionable comfort. Our rooms are designed for your comfort and convenience. Your security is our primary concern and you'll find our customer care second to none.

In addition to this, we also have the best Golf Resort center where you will be able to relax, enjoy yourself, either you're visiting, looking for a place to tee-off the day's stress, just for relaxation or a golf enthusiast searching for an international standard golf course, you really can't look past Smokin Hills . We are here to serve you.

OUR VISION

To Provide World Class Convenient Hotel for people in Africa where they could relax & enjoy themselves.

OUR MISSION

To make sure we continue to blend to the future hospitality sector.
