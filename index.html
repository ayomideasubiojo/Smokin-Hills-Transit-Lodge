ABOUT US

Smokin Hills Transit Lodge is the most preferred hotel in Nigeria due to its golf resort and it's all about the right mix! located on the outskirt of the quiet town of Ilara Mokin, 20 minutes drive from Akure the capital city of Ondo state. We offer our corporate clients and walk in guests a perfect blend of relaxation,where modern style meets unquestionable comfort.
